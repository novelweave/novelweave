Privacy Policy


Welcome to Novel Weave Aari and Embroidery Shop! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with us:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing and shipping address.
  • Order Details: Measurements, design preferences, and customization requests.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, and usage data collected through cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To improve our products, services, and website functionality.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your data in the following scenarios:

  • With trusted third-party service providers who assist in payment processing, delivery, or other operational tasks.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies help us analyze website traffic, remember your preferences, and enhance site performance. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no online platform can be 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • The right to withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • The right to file a complaint with a data protection authority.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revision date. Please review it periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
